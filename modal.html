Flagship Laptops Battle of 2023: HUAWEI MateBook X Pro Vs. 14-inch Apple MacBook Pro

Image Credit: Midjourney
Huawei just announced the latest addition to its MateBook series, the HUAWEI MateBook X Pro, as the latest smart flagship laptop from the iconic tech brand. So, we think it is the right time to compare it against another popular laptop of the same class, the 14-inch MacBook Pro M2. Not only is it going to be fun, but it will also help you look at all the features side by side and figure out the right laptop to choose from. Let’s get started without further ado.
Display and Design

The display on the new HUAWEI MateBook X Pro is outstanding. The 14.2-inch 3.1K HUAWEI Real Colour FullView Display has a whopping screen-to-body ratio of 92.5%. The colour accuracy of 1 with support for both the P3 and sRGB dual colour gamut means that you can use this laptop for colour-sensitive tasks like photo and video editing. Furthermore, the Magnetically-controlled Nano Optical AR Layer available on the Premium Edition effectively reduces reflection for greater clarity, even in extremely bright environments.

MateBook X Pro redefines PC aesthetics with new material processing techniques. It sports a skin-soothing metallic body, delivering a unique matte surface and offering a comfortable grip. The Premium Edition of the HUAWEI MateBook X Pro’s body is made of lightweight and strong magnesium alloy. The innovative Micro-arc Oxidation Coating process is applied to the magnesium alloy airy body, which not only improves the hardness and wear resistance of the product body but also makes the production process more environmentally friendly.

The Breville features a Liquid Retina XDR Display that uses mini LEDs, which should give better picture quality than traditional LCD screens. This is a non-touch display, though. The bezels around the screen are smaller. However, there is a notch at the top of the screen for housing the 1080p web camera. This time around, the laptop has actually gotten a tad bit thicker because of the inclusion of more ports. Now it comes with three Thunderbolt 4 ports, an SD card slot, an HDMI port that supports 8K, the MagSafe 3 charging port, and a headphone jack.

Screen and aesthetics-wise, we think that the HUAWEI MateBook X Pro is a game changer!

Performance

Featuring a powerful 13th Gen Intel Core processor in its lightweight body, the performance of the new HUAWEI MateBook X Pro is the best in class. The fact that all versions of the HUAWEI MateBook X Pro have been certified by Intel Evo is a testimony to its capabilities. But if you want even more power to handle multiple complex tasks simultaneously, you can enable the 30W Performance Mode by pressing the Fn + P key. The laptop supports up to 32 GB LPDDR5 5600 MHz dual-channel high-speed memory and supports up to 2 TB NVMe PCIe SSD.

The 14-inch HUAWEI MateBook Pro comes with two options for processors; you can either choose the model with M2 Pro or the more expensive one featuring M2 Max. Compared to the M2 Pro, the beefier M2 Max offers better graphics performance with all the extra GPU cores.

Smart Features

The HUAWEI MateBook X Pro take multi-device collaboration to a whole new level, thanks to Super Device, bringing super productivity with a cohesive multi-device experience. It lets you effortlessly connect to other Huawei devices nearby and collaborate between them all. Simply place your HUAWEI FreeBuds 5, mouse, keyboard, speakers and printers close to the HUAWEI MateBook X Pro, and the laptop will automatically discover them and provide a prompt for quick and easy pairing.

With tablets like the HUAWEI MatePad Pro, it is even more useful. In Mirror Mode, the laptop screen is projected onto the tablet, with both devices showing the same content: the tablet can benefit from the laptop’s incredible performance, while the laptop can access the tablet’s stylus, making drawing even easier. With Extend Mode, the laptop display is extended to the tablet, allowing the tablet to serve as an external monitor so you can scroll and browse easily between the two – perfect for making notes during a class or meeting. Finally, Collaborate Mode lets you share content between the devices via a simple drag and drop, so anything left on one can easily be transferred to the other. Notably, you can control your tablet with your fingers in any of these modes without having to rely on a stylus for everything.

Apple offers something called Continuity that helps tie together multiple Apple devices, like Macs, iPhones, and iPads. Notably, this feature allows you to connect between an iPad and the MacBook to use it as an extra display or collaborate together. You will be able to mirror your MacBook display to the iPad and use the tablet as an input device for the laptop using the Apple Pencil but not your fingers. The tablet can also extend the laptop’s display, and you will be able to drag and drop files between the devices simultaneously. And you can control both devices with a single keyboard and mouse. Continuity also allows you to mirror your tablet display on the MacBook screen.

Overall, the implementation of the features feels a lot more complete and more seamless on HUAWEI MateBook Super Device. Also, with the flexibility of the Windows ecosystem and seamless integration of multiple devices, Super Device offers the best of both worlds.

Verdict

When you weigh all the features, it is easy to see that the HUAWEI MateBook X Pro gives you the best flagship experience. It boasts some unmatchable features; the 3.1K HUAWEI Real Colour FullView Display has all the resolution you want, the processor is powerful enough to handle anything you throw at it, and the visionary Super Device features make it easier to manage multiple devices. So, if what you want is an all-rounded and stylish laptop that you can use for everything, including productivity and entertainment, HUAWEI MateBook X Pro is the right choice.
